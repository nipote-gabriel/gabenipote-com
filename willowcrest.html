Willowcrest Partners

Content & strategy for growing companies.

What I Do

I help companies grow. I focus on go-to-market, and where content meets strategy.

My Approach

I believe every company needs three narratives:

  • Vision — what you're building and why it matters
  • Internal — the story your team tells itself about how you're going to get there
  • External — how you tell that story to the market, customers, partners, and investors

I help companies build and tell these stories.

Companies I've Worked With

I've been lucky to advise some great startups, including frank & beans pictures, CareQB, Doggy Paddle Whiskey, Super Ego Studio, and more. I've worked with these founders to get work featured in Ad Age, close initial recurring revenue, and land contracts with blue chip customers like Adobe, Notion, and The Browser Company.
